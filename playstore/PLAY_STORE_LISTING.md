## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
🎮 QIXIT — The arcade classic, reimagined.

Draw lines, claim territory, and reveal stunning backgrounds — but watch out for the Qix and the Sparx! One wrong move and it's game over.

QIXIT is a loving homage to the legendary 1981 arcade game QIX, rebuilt from scratch with modern visuals, dynamic backgrounds, and deep customization. Whether you're a retro gaming veteran or discovering territorial arcade games for the first time, QIXIT delivers pure, addictive gameplay in a beautiful package.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
⚡ HOW TO PLAY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• Move along the border walls with arrow keys or swipe controls
• Hold to draw a trail into unclaimed territory
• Return to the border to close your shape and claim the area
• Claim 75%+ of the field to complete each level
• Avoid the Qix (bouncing enemy) and Sparx (border patrols) — they'll end your run!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
✨ FEATURES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

🖼️ DYNAMIC BACKGROUNDS
Each level reveals a different background image as you claim territory. Choose from multiple themes:
  • Landscapes — curated real photography
  • Space — NASA nebulae, galaxies & cosmos
  • Anime — SFW anime art
  • City — urban & architectural photography
  • Nature — outdoor & natural scenery
  • Art — public domain paintings from the Met Museum
  • Custom — search with your own keywords

🎵 30+ MUSIC TRACKS & SOUND EFFECTS
A rich library of background music and customizable sound effects. Build your own playlist, assign effects to game events, and preview everything in the Advanced Options panel.

🎨 DEEP CUSTOMIZATION
  • Customize the Qix enemy appearance — adjust segments, arm length, rotation speed, glow, and color mode
  • Swap player, enemy, and Sparx sprites
  • Import/export complete theme configurations
  • Bonus collectible objects with custom sprites

🌟 PROGRESSIVE DIFFICULTY
  • 20+ levels with increasing fill targets
  • More enemies and faster Sparx as you advance
  • NASA "Astronomy Picture of the Day" as a special level background
  • Star rating system (1–3 stars based on area claimed)

🏆 POLISHED GAME FEEL
  • Smooth 60fps HTML5 Canvas rendering
  • Particle explosions and screen effects
  • Level completion celebrations with confetti
  • Life-loss flash effects and invulnerability grace period
  • Responsive HUD with glassmorphism design

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🕹️ CONTROLS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Arrow keys / WASD — Move player
Space / Hold direction — Start drawing
ESC — Pause / Resume
Menu button — Return to main menu

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Free to play. No ads (yet). Made with ❤️ by Roberto Zanardo.

Powered by free APIs: NASA APOD, Picsum Photos, nekos.best, Metropolitan Museum of Art, Wikimedia Commons, Safebooru, and LoremFlickr.
```

## Notes

- **Privacy Policy**: Google requires a privacy policy URL. You can use a free generator or host a simple page on GitHub Pages.
- **Screenshots**: The generated screenshots are AI mockups of the game's actual screens. For the best listing, consider also taking real device screenshots from the running app using `adb shell screencap`.
- **Feature Graphic**: Displayed at the top of your Play Store listing. Keep it simple and impactful.
- **Icon**: The adaptive icon version should be added to `android/app/src/main/res/mipmap-*` folders. Use Android Studio's Image Asset tool to generate all density variants from the 512×512 source.
