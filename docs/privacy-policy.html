QIXIT

Privacy Policy

Effective: April 4, 2026

1. Introduction

QIXIT ("the App") is a free-to-play arcade game developed by Roberto Zanardo ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

In short: QIXIT does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game runs entirely on your device.

2. Data We Collect

QIXIT does not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or registration — You can play without creating an account
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics SDK or tracking tools
  • No advertising — The App currently contains no ads or ad-related tracking
  • No location data — We do not access your device's location
  • No personal identifiers — We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or device IDs

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores game preferences and progress (such as your selected theme, sound settings, and level completion status) locally on your device using standard browser/WebView storage mechanisms (localStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Can be cleared by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the App

4. Third-Party Services

QIXIT fetches images from the following free, public APIs to display dynamic background artwork during gameplay:

  • NASA APOD — Astronomy Picture of the Day
  • Picsum Photos — Random photography
  • nekos.best — Anime art
  • Metropolitan Museum of Art API — Public domain artwork
  • Wikimedia Commons — Free media
  • Safebooru — Safe anime images
  • LoremFlickr — Themed stock photos

These requests fetch images only. No personal data is sent to these services beyond what is included in a standard HTTP request (such as your IP address, which is handled by the service's own privacy policy). We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services if you have concerns.

5. Children's Privacy

QIXIT is rated for all ages (Everyone / PEGI 3). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or any other users. Since no data is collected at all, the App complies with applicable children's privacy regulations including COPPA.

6. Future Changes

If we introduce features that involve data collection in the future (such as ads, leaderboards, or analytics), we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly and notify users through an App update. We will always seek your consent where required by law.

7. Your Rights

Since QIXIT does not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. If you have any concerns or questions, you are welcome to contact us.
